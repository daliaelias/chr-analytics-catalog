Specialized Service — Requires Analytics Team Involvement

This service is designed for clinics with active research programs, complex data reporting needs, or data quality issues affecting clinical decision-making. It involves collaboration with our dedicated analytics team and is scoped individually based on your clinic's data environment. Not all clinics will require this service.

When your CHR reports aren't producing accurate numbers, the problem is rarely the report itself — it's how data is being entered upstream. Our Business Analysts trace the issue back through your workflows, identify where and why incorrect data is being captured, and work with your analytics team to fix both the process and the output.

Diagnostic Phase

  • Report accuracy review & discrepancy mapping
  • Data entry workflow tracing (source to output)
  • Root cause identification (human, process, or config)
  • Stakeholder interviews (clinical + admin + analytics)

Resolution & Handoff

  • Workflow correction recommendations
  • Collaboration with your analytics/data team
  • Staff re-training on corrected data entry
  • Validation report confirming data accuracy

Ideal For

  • Clinics with active research or data mining programs
  • Multi-provider clinics with inconsistent reporting
  • Organizations preparing for accreditation or audit
  • Clinics where billing data doesn't match clinical data

What Makes This Different

  • BA-led investigation (not just IT or training)
  • Bridges the gap between clinical workflow & data output
  • Works alongside your existing analytics team
  • Fixes the root cause, not just the symptom
