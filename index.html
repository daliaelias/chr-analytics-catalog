Our analytics team offers a range of services to help your clinic get the most out of your data — from quick fixes and dashboard updates to complex custom reporting and training.

Services are available to all clients, with some services reserved for Advanced, Enterprise, or Alberta PCN packages. Your CARE team will confirm which services are included in your package and determine the scope and any applicable costs after your request is submitted.
