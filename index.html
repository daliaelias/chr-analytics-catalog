📋 How to Submit Your Analytics Request

A service-by-service guide on what information to include when emailing the CARE team — featuring a filled example on the left and a blank copy-paste template on the right for each request type.

Last updated: April 2026
Ready? Find your service below, copy the blank template, fill in your details, and email the CARE team. If the email button doesn't open, use the Copy Email button and paste the address manually.

To get the fastest turnaround on your analytics request, it's important to include the right information when you email the CARE team. This guide walks you through exactly what to include for each type of request.

Each service below has two columns: a filled example on the left so you can see what a complete request looks like, and a blank template on the right that you can copy, paste into your email, and fill in. Send your completed email to care.emraccounts@telus.com.

📌

These two fields are included in every request template below — don't forget them!

  • Your clinic domain / URL (e.g., myclinic.teluschr.com)
  • Your name and email address
