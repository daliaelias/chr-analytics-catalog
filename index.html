⚡ Quick Fixes
Available to all clients  ·  No BA review required  ·  Resolved within 5 business days
🐛 Bug or Error
All Clients Est. 60–120 min

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL (e.g., myclinic.teluschr.com)
  • Your name and email address
  • Name of the affected dashboard or report
  • Description of the error or unexpected behaviour
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (what did you click/do?)
  • When the issue started (date/time if known)
  • Screenshot or screen recording of the error
  • Expected vs. actual behaviour
  • Mark subject line as: Bug/Error

📧 Example Email

Subject: Bug/Error – [Dashboard Name] Hi CARE Team, I'm writing to report a bug on our analytics dashboard. Domain: myclinic.teluschr.com Contact: Jane Smith (jane.smith@myclinic.com) Dashboard: Patient Appointments Overview Issue Description: The date range filter is not working correctly. When I select "Last 30 Days," the dashboard shows data from 6 months ago instead of the current period. Steps to Reproduce: 1. Open the Patient Appointments Overview dashboard 2. Click the date filter dropdown 3. Select "Last 30 Days" 4. Dashboard refreshes but shows incorrect date range When it started: Noticed on April 10, 2026 Expected: Data from March 11 – April 10, 2026 Actual: Data from October 2025 I've attached a screenshot showing the issue. Please let me know if you need any additional information. Thank you, Jane Smith

📊 Dashboard Issues
All Clients Est. 60–90 min

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL
  • Your name and email address
  • Name of the affected dashboard
  • What was working before vs. what is broken now
  • Which specific element is not working (filter, tile, chart, date range)
  • When the issue started
  • Screenshot showing the problem

📧 Example Email

Subject: Dashboard Issue – [Dashboard Name] Hi CARE Team, I'm experiencing an issue with one of our dashboards. Domain: myclinic.teluschr.com Contact: John Doe (john.doe@myclinic.com) Dashboard: Monthly Billing Summary Issue: The "Provider" filter dropdown is no longer showing all providers. Previously it showed 8 providers; now it only shows 2. This was working correctly as of last week (April 7, 2026). No changes were made on our end. I've attached a screenshot for reference. Thank you, John Doe

✏️ Minor Dashboard Updates
All Clients Est. 60–90 min

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL
  • Your name and email address
  • Name of the dashboard to be updated
  • Specific change(s) requested (title, label, description, typo fix)
  • Current text/value and what it should be changed to

Add a simple filter for "Appointment Type" (data comes from the Appointments section in CHR) No structural changes needed — just these three small updates. Thank you, Sarah Lee

📈 New Simple Custom Dashboard
All Clients Est. 60–120 min  ·  Max 9 tiles, 29 columns
💡
Tip: More detail = faster turnaround The more specific you are about your data source, tile types, and metrics, the faster the CARE team can scope and schedule your request. If you have an Excel mockup, attach it to your email.

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL
  • Your name and email address
  • Clinic or program name
  • Dashboard name / title
  • Goal: what question should this dashboard answer?
  • Data source: which CHR section(s) your data comes from (e.g., Appointments, Questionnaires, Demographics)
  • For each data source: specific field names, filters, and date range
  • Number of tiles needed (max 9)
  • For each tile: name, type (table, chart, count, etc.), and metrics/fields to show
  • Any filters the dashboard should have (e.g., by provider, date, status)
  • Reference dashboard or mockup (if available)

📧 Example Email

Subject: New Simple Custom Dashboard – [Dashboard Name] Hi CARE Team, I'd like to request a new custom dashboard for our clinic. Domain: myclinic.teluschr.com Contact: Lisa Park (l.park@myclinic.com) Clinic: Menopause Wellness Clinic Dashboard Name: Patient Symptom Tracking – Pre/Post Goal: Track patient symptom severity scores before and after treatment to measure program effectiveness. Data Source: - CHR Section: Questionnaire Responses - Questionnaire Name: "Menopause Clinic Symptom Severity Questionnaire" (exact name in CHR) - Fields needed: Patient name, DOB, Pre-treatment score, Post-treatment score, Date completed - Date range: All responses from Jan 1, 2025 onward - Filter: Active patients only Dashboard Tiles (3 tiles total): Tile 1: Total Patients with Pre & Post Scores - Type: Single Count / Scorecard - Metric: Count of patients who have both a pre and post questionnaire response Tile 2: Average Score Comparison - Type: Bar Chart - Metric: Average pre-treatment score vs. average post-treatment score, side by side Tile 3: Patient Detail Table - Type: Table - Columns: Patient Name, DOB, Pre Score, Post Score, Score Change, Provider - Sort: By Score Change (descending) Filters needed: - Date range filter (by questionnaire completion date) - Provider filter Reference: I've attached an Excel mockup showing the layout I have in mind. Thank you, Lisa Park

🔧 Modify Existing Dashboard
All Clients Est. 60–120 min  ·  One page of requirements

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL
  • Your name and email address
  • Exact name of the dashboard to be modified
  • List of specific changes requested (be as precise as possible)
  • For new tiles: name, type, metrics, and data source
  • For edits to existing tiles: current state and desired state
  • Any new filters to add
  • Screenshot of the current dashboard with annotations (recommended)

📧 Example Email

Subject: Modify Existing Dashboard – [Dashboard Name] Hi CARE Team, I'd like to request modifications to an existing dashboard. Domain: myclinic.teluschr.com Contact: Tom Rivera (t.rivera@myclinic.com) Dashboard: Appointment Summary – 2026 Requested Changes: Change 1 – Edit existing tile: Tile: "Total Appointments" Current: Shows all appointment types combined Requested: Break out by appointment type (New Patient, Follow-Up, No-Show) Change 2 – Add new tile: Name: Cancellation Rate by Provider Type: Bar Chart Metric: % of appointments cancelled, grouped by provider Data: Appointments section in CHR, filter: Status = Cancelled Date range: Same as rest of dashboard Change 3 – Add filter: Add a "Location" filter to the dashboard (data from Appointments > Location field) I've attached a screenshot of the current dashboard with my annotations. Thank you, Tom Rivera

🏗️ New Complex Custom Dashboard
All Clients ⚠️ BA Review Est. 240–360 min  ·  10+ tiles or 30+ columns

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL
  • Your name and email address
  • Clinic or program name
  • Dashboard name and overall goal
  • All data sources (CHR sections, field names, filters, date ranges)
  • Number of tiles (10+) with details for each
  • For each tile: name, type, metrics, groupings, filters
  • Any calculated fields or custom logic needed
  • Whether LookML changes may be required
  • Dashboard-level filters needed
  • Mockup, wireframe, or Excel example (strongly recommended)
  • Timeline or deadline (if applicable)

📧 Example Email

Subject: New Complex Custom Dashboard – [Dashboard Name] Hi CARE Team, I'd like to request a new complex dashboard for our PCN reporting. Domain: pcnclinic.teluschr.com Contact: Dr. Aisha Patel (a.patel@pcnclinic.com) Clinic: Northern Alberta PCN Dashboard Name: PCN Chronic Disease Management Report 2026 Goal: Comprehensive reporting on chronic disease management across all 5 clinics in our PCN — tracking patient panels, encounter frequency, medication adherence, and outcome scores. Data Sources: 1. Demographics – Patient name, DOB, gender, status, assigned provider, clinic location 2. Encounters – Encounter type, date, provider, reason (filter: chronic disease encounters only) 3. Prescriptions – Medication name, status, prescriber (filter: active medications for diabetes/hypertension) 4. Custom Patient Data – Category: Chronic Disease, Property: Risk Score, Field: Diabetes Risk Level 5. Questionnaire Responses – "Chronic Disease Outcomes Questionnaire" – Pre/Post scores Tiles needed (12 total): [See attached requirements document for full tile specifications] Summary of tiles: - 3 scorecards (total patients, avg encounters/patient, avg outcome score) - 4 bar charts (by provider, by clinic, by disease type, by risk level) - 2 line graphs (encounter trend over time, outcome score trend) - 2 tables (patient detail list, provider summary) - 1 pie chart (disease type distribution) Dashboard filters: Date range, Clinic/Location, Provider, Disease Type, Risk Level Custom logic needed: - Calculate "Encounter Frequency" = total encounters / months since enrollment - Flag patients with no encounter in last 90 days I've attached a detailed requirements document and an Excel mockup. Timeline: Needed before our PCN board meeting on May 15, 2026. Thank you, Dr. Aisha Patel

⚙️ Complex Modifications to Existing Dashboard
All Clients ⚠️ BA Review Est. 120–240 min  ·  LookML changes may apply

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your clinic domain / URL
  • Your name and email address
  • Exact name of the dashboard to be modified
  • Full list of changes (multiple pages of requirements expected)
  • For each change: current state and desired state
  • Any new data sources or fields not currently in the dashboard
  • Whether custom calculations or LookML changes are needed
  • Annotated screenshot of current dashboard
  • Mockup or wireframe of desired outcome

📧 Example Email

Subject: Complex Dashboard Modification – [Dashboard Name] Hi CARE Team, I'd like to request significant modifications to an existing dashboard. Domain: myclinic.teluschr.com Contact: James Wu (j.wu@myclinic.com) Dashboard: Billing & Revenue Summary 2025 Overview of changes: This dashboard needs to be substantially restructured to support our new reporting requirements. The changes involve adding new data sources, restructuring existing tiles, and adding calculated fields. Changes requested: 1. Add new data source: Insured Billing section - New fields: Claim Status, Payer, Amount Billed, Amount Paid - New calculated field: Collection Rate = Amount Paid / Amount Billed 2. Restructure "Revenue by Provider" tile: - Current: Simple bar chart showing total billed - New: Grouped bar chart showing Billed vs. Paid side by side, by provider - Add: Trend line overlay showing monthly collection rate 3. Add 3 new tiles (see attached requirements doc for full specs) 4. Add dashboard-level filters: Payer Type, Claim Status, Billing Period Note: Some of these changes may require LookML modifications as the Insured Billing data model may need to be extended. I've attached a detailed requirements document with annotated screenshots. Thank you, James Wu

🌐 Multi-Domain Reporting
🌐 Alberta PCN Only ⚠️ BA Review Est. TBA  ·  BA scoping required

✅ What to Include in Your Email

  • Your PCN name and all clinic domains to be included
  • Your name and email address
  • Number of clinics/domains to aggregate
  • Which user(s) will have cross-domain access
  • What data needs to be reported across all domains
  • Whether you need a new dashboard or modifications to existing ones
  • Any clinic-specific vs. aggregate-level reporting needs
  • Timeline or reporting deadline

📧 Example Email

Subject: Multi-Domain Reporting Request – [PCN Name] Hi CARE Team, I'd like to request multi-domain reporting setup for our PCN. PCN Name: Northern Alberta Primary Care Network Contact: Rachel Thompson (r.thompson@northernpcn.ca) Clinics to include: 1. northclinic1.teluschr.com 2. northclinic2.teluschr.com 3. northclinic3.teluschr.com 4. northclinic4.teluschr.com Cross-domain user: Rachel Thompson (r.thompson@northernpcn.ca) This user should be able to view and report across all 4 domains. Reporting needs: - Aggregate patient counts across all clinics - Encounter volumes by clinic and by provider - Chronic disease panel management metrics - Comparison view: clinic vs. clinic performance New dashboard needed: Yes – "PCN Network Overview Dashboard" (Separate consultation request will follow for dashboard specs) Timeline: Setup needed by May 1, 2026 ahead of our Q2 board reporting. Please let me know what additional information is needed for the BA scoping call. Thank you, Rachel Thompson
