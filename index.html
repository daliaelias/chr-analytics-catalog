Subject: New Complex Custom Dashboard – PCN Chronic Disease Report Hi CARE Team, I'd like to request a new complex dashboard for our PCN reporting. Domain: pcnclinic.teluschr.com Contact: Dr. Aisha Patel (a.patel@pcnclinic.com) Clinic: Northern Alberta PCN Dashboard Name: PCN Chronic Disease Management Report 2026 Goal: Comprehensive reporting on chronic disease management across all 5 clinics — tracking patient panels, encounter frequency, medication adherence, and outcome scores. Data Sources: 1. Demographics – Patient name, DOB, gender, status, assigned provider, clinic location 2. Encounters – Encounter type, date, provider, reason (filter: chronic disease encounters only) 3. Prescriptions – Medication name, status, prescriber 4. Questionnaire Responses – "Chronic Disease Outcomes Questionnaire" – Pre/Post scores Tiles needed (12 total): - 3 scorecards (total patients, avg encounters/patient, avg outcome score) - 4 bar charts (by provider, by clinic, by disease type, by risk level) - 2 line graphs (encounter trend over time, outcome score trend) - 2 tables (patient detail list, provider summary) - 1 pie chart (disease type distribution) Dashboard filters: Date range, Clinic/Location, Provider, Disease Type, Risk Level Custom logic needed: - Calculate "Encounter Frequency" = total encounters / months since enrollment - Flag patients with no encounter in last 90 days I've attached a detailed requirements document and an Excel mockup. Timeline: Needed before our PCN board meeting on May 15, 2026. Thank you, Dr. Aisha Patel
